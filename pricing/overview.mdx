## What's Included

All paid plans include:

- **Google Search Console integration** - Track organic search performance
- **Google Analytics 4 integration** - Monitor website traffic and user behavior
- **PageSpeed Insights** - Analyze site performance metrics
- **AI-powered insights** - Claude API generates actionable recommendations
- **PDF report generation** - Professional, branded reports for clients
- **Custom branding** - Company name and primary color customization

## White-Label Branding

White-label branding removes Reportr branding from generated reports and allows you to customize:

- Company logo on reports
- Custom primary color scheme
- Company name throughout the interface
- Support email on reports

**Availability:**
- **Free & Starter**: Not available (Starter users can add for $20/month)
- **Professional & Agency**: Included in plan

<Info>
  During trial periods, white-label branding is temporarily enabled for all plans to allow testing.
</Info>

## Fair Usage Policy

Reportr uses a **30-day rolling billing cycle** to track usage fairly. Your report limit resets automatically every 30 days from your subscription start date, not on calendar months.

See [Billing Cycles](/pricing/billing-cycles) for detailed information on how usage tracking works.

## Choosing the Right Plan

Consider these factors when selecting a plan:

1. **Number of active clients** - Count clients you'll manage simultaneously
2. **Report frequency** - Estimate how many reports you'll generate per month
3. **White-label requirements** - Determine if branded reports are essential
4. **Growth trajectory** - Choose a plan with room to grow

<Tip>
  Start with a smaller plan and upgrade as needed. You can change plans at any time, and upgrades take effect immediately.
</Tip>
